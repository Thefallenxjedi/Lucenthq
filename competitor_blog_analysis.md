## 🔑 Common Keywords & Themes

After reviewing the content across all these platforms, here are the core keywords and themes that every single competitor is targeting right now:

### 1. **AI (Artificial Intelligence)**
Every company is rapidly pivoting to talk about how AI enhances their product. 
*   *Common phrasing:* "AI-powered root cause analysis", "AI-driven UX", "AI ticket generation".
*   **Takeaway for Lucent:** Because AI is Lucent's core foundation (rather than an add-on), Lucent should lean heavily into positioning itself as "AI-native" rather than just having "AI features."

### 2. **Session Replay / Recordings**
The term "Session Replay" is ubiquitous. It has transitioned from a nice-to-have UX tool into a mandatory observability standard for debugging.

### 3. **Observability vs. Analytics**
There is a massive industry shift from traditional "Analytics" (numbers and charts) to "Observability" (understanding *why* something happened). All blogs emphasize moving beyond simple error tracking into full context visibility.

### 4. **Friction / Rage Clicks**
Understanding user emotion and struggle is a massive keyword cluster. Words like "Frustration," "Rage Clicks," "Dead Clicks," and "Friction Points" appear constantly across FullStory, LogRocket, and PostHog.

### 5. **Reproducibility ("Cannot Reproduce")**
A major pain point targeted by Sentry, LogRocket, and Jam.dev. The goal is to give developers exact steps, logs, and network requests so they never have to guess how a bug happened.
