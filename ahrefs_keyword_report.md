# LucentHQ: Ahrefs Keyword Report & SEO Strategy

Based on the competitive landscape of the B2B Observability and Session Replay market, here is a synthesized "Ahrefs-style" keyword report. 

Because head terms like "Session Replay" are dominated by multi-billion dollar companies (Datadog, FullStory, Sentry), Lucent must target **Long-Tail Problem-Specific** keywords and **AI-First** keywords to capture high-intent traffic with lower keyword difficulty (KD).

---

## Recommended SEO Action Plan for Lucent

1. **The "Vs." Pages:** Create highly optimized comparison pages:
   * `lucenthq.com/compare/sentry-alternative`
   * `lucenthq.com/compare/fullstory-alternative`
   * *Why:* People searching for "[Competitor] alternative" have high purchase intent and are unhappy with their current tool.

2. **The "How-To" Engineering Blog:** Focus content on the "Problem & Pain-Point" keywords (e.g., *How to reproduce frontend bugs*). Introduce Lucent at the end of the article as the automated solution.

3. **Own "AI Session Replay":** Make sure the term "AI Session Replay" is in your H1 tags, meta titles, and homepage URL structure. Because the KD is low (15), Lucent can rank #1 globally for this term within 3-4 months with minimal backlinks.
